THE TECHNOLOGY NEED IN KAVERI ENGINE

The Kaveri engine,developed by India's Gas Turbine Research Establishment (GTRE) within the defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO),is undergoing trials at the Gromov Flight Research Institute (GFRI) in Russia, marking a significant advancement in the country's aerospace capabilities.The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)is conducting detailed trials with approximately 25 hours of testing remaining,while simultaneously preparing for extensive 70-hour flight trials on a Russian Ilyuhin Il-76 test bed aircraft.
